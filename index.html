2048 is a free online number puzzle. Slide tiles to merge matching numbers and reach the 2048 tile. Features 5 themes, AI auto-play, a robot guide that explains every move, antigravity mode, and a full strategy guide. No download needed — works on desktop and mobile.

Use arrow keys, WASD, or swipe to move. When two tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one. Build toward 2048!
